# Privacy Policy — Airship Piano

**Last updated: March 5, 2026**

Airship Piano does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the app does
- Connects to MIDI keyboards via USB or Bluetooth
- Downloads a piano soundfont file to your device for local playback
- Plays audio locally on your device

## Data collection
- **No analytics** are collected
- **No personal information** is collected
- **No data** is sent to any server (except the initial soundfont download)
- **No accounts** are required
- **No cookies or tracking** of any kind

## Bluetooth
The app uses Bluetooth solely to connect to MIDI keyboards. No Bluetooth data is collected or transmitted.

## Soundfont download
On first launch, the app downloads a piano soundfont file from the internet. This is a one-time download stored locally on your device. No personal data is sent during this download.
