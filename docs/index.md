# Airship Piano

A lightweight, open-source MIDI piano app. Connect your MIDI keyboard and just play.

- Works with USB and Bluetooth MIDI keyboards
- High-quality Yamaha C5 Grand Piano sound (Salamander soundfont)
- Multi-keyboard support with per-device transpose
- Available on iOS and macOS
