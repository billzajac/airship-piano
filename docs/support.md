# Support — Airship Piano

## Getting Started

1. Connect a MIDI keyboard via USB or Bluetooth
2. Open Airship Piano
3. Play!

On first launch, the app downloads a high-quality piano soundfont (~24 MB). This is a one-time download.

## Bluetooth MIDI (iOS)

Tap the **Bluetooth MIDI** button to scan for and connect Bluetooth MIDI keyboards.

## Multiple Keyboards

Airship Piano supports multiple MIDI keyboards simultaneously. Each device gets its own transpose controls.

## Troubleshooting

**No sound?**
- Check that your device volume is turned up
- Check the volume slider in the app
- Make sure your MIDI keyboard is connected (green dot next to device name)

**Soundfont download failed?**
- Check your internet connection and tap Retry
- On macOS, you can use the built-in General MIDI sound as a fallback

**MIDI keyboard not detected?**
- Unplug and replug the USB cable
- For Bluetooth: make sure the keyboard is in pairing mode
- Try restarting the app
